About Me

I am currently a second year MASc student studying at the University of Toronto, supverised by Prof. Scott Sanner. My research direction is on Conversational Recommender Systems (CRSs). This is focused on building and evaluating CRSs using machine learning tools such as Bayesian approach, deep learning, and language models

I am working at Loblaw Digital as a Data Scientist intern. Before that, I interned at the Vector Institute as an applied machine learning intern, also interned as a data engineer intern at RBC , and I worked as a Java Developer at Intact Financial Corporation during my professional experience yar.

Prior to that, I completed my BASc. in Industrial Engineering at the University of Toronto, where I got chance to work with Prof. Birsen Donmez as a research assistant and completed my capstone project with Prof. Scott Sanner in the final year, which won the first-place capstone design award.
